ABOUT I'VE GOT GAS

Step into the world of Gavin's Gas Installation, a premier establishment located in the vibrant heart of Johannesburg. Our commitment to exceptional gas solutions resonates with the dynamic spirit of this bustling city, blending a touch of urban sophistication with our specialized services. Nestled in the energy of Johannesburg, we take pride in offering unparalleled expertise in gas water heaters, stoves, gas fireplaces, as well as comprehensive maintenance, servicing, and COC inspections.

We are certified specialists in:

DOMESTIC INSTALLATIONS

We are experts in all aspects of gas installations, and can provide you with the best possible solution for your needs. We understand the importance of safety when it comes to gas installations. We are certified to the highest standards in gas safety and up-to-date with the latest regulations and safety requirements.

CERTIFICATES OF COMPLIANCE

We are certified and licensed to carry out domestic installations safely. All our installations and modifications are pressure tested for compliance and gas leak prevention.

REPAIRS AND MAINTENANCE

We specialise in repairs and maintenance for residential settings. We understand the importance of having a fully functioning system and strive to provide our customers with the best service possible.

ANNUAL SERVICES

We offer professional gas servicing which covers everything from a safety check and servicing of all gas appliances, to a full gas safety certificate. We check for any signs of gas leaks, corrosion, or other problems, and provide you with a detailed report of the results.
